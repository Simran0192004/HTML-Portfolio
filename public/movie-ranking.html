The 3 Best Movies According to Simran Singh

My Top 3 Movies of All Times

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara

It's not movie. It's an emotion. It inspires us to let go of all the fears and live life like you're dying tomorrow.

PK

One of the most light-hearted yet inspiring story of an Alien stuck on Earth.

Veere Di Wedding

The best movie for a girl's night. It's a story of about four girl friends and them coping with their chaotic life.
